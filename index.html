About Us

The Cascades Tech Club was founded to provide students with a social network of individuals interested in computer science. The club was founded in Spring 2016 by a small group of students seeking to organize students in the growing Computer Science program at OSU-Cascades.

The club's faculty advisor is Dr. Marc Rubin.


Get Involved!

If you're interested in getting involved with the Cascades Tech Club, visit the Contact Us page to contact one of our board members, or to sign up for our mailing list. Our mailing list is used to announce club events, meetings, and inform students of Computer Sceince related internships or job opportunities within our community. The fastest way to get in touch with us is through Discord. Someone is always online.

The club is always looking for new members; we look forward to hearing from you!
